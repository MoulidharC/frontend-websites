Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who, by his sheer grit and hard work, achieves success should share his story with the rest, for they may find inspiration and strength. "Wings of Fire" is an autobiography by the visionary scientist Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. From humble beginnings, he rose to become the President of India. This book is filled with life experiences, personal stories, and valuable insights from his remarkable journey.

The 3 Mistakes of My Life

by Chetan Bhagat

"The 3 Mistakes of My Life" is the third novel by the famous Indian author Chetan Bhagat. Based on cricket, business, and religion, the story is set in Ahmedabad. It revolves around three young men — Omi, Ishaan, and Govind — who are trying to achieve success. Based on real events, the book begins with a dramatic email sent by Govind, setting the tone for an emotional and inspiring journey.

Harry Potter

by J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter's life is miserable. His parents are dead, and he is forced to live with his cruel relatives who make him stay in a small room under the stairs. His life changes when he receives a letter revealing the truth — he is a wizard. A mysterious visitor rescues him and takes him to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where Harry finally begins to feel like he truly belongs.
